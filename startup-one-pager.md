## The Problem

Sales reps, founders, and executives spend 3–5 hours preparing for a single important meeting — researching attendees on LinkedIn, cobbling together talking points, guessing what objections will come up, and writing agendas from scratch. Most still walk in underprepared. The result: stalled deals, missed closes, and wasted relationship capital.

**$47B** is lost annually in the US alone due to failed sales meetings.

---

## The Solution: MeetingMind AI

An AI agent that delivers a complete, personalized meeting briefing in under 60 seconds.

Given a meeting title, type, goal, and attendee names — MeetingMind autonomously generates:

- **Attendee Profiles** — inferred role, interests, pain points, and conversation hooks
- **Strategic Agenda** — time-boxed blocks optimized for the specific meeting goal
- **Talking Points** — specific phrases and tactics, not generic advice
- **Predicted Q&A** — the 5 hardest questions + battle-tested word-for-word answers
- **Pre-Meeting Checklist** — prioritized actions with time estimates
